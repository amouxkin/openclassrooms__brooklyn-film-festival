The Metroplex Proudly Present 2018 Broklyn Film Festival




Tuesday, August 21st, 2018


Every year the Brooklyn Film Festival donates hundreds of complementary tickets to Brooklyn Schools and offers to Students & Senior Citizens discounted tickets: $12 instead of $15. One of BFF' s oldest programs, KidsFilmFest, an event for and by young filmmakers and film buffs, this year held at Made in NY Media Center by IFP, will be completely free of charge for all the kids and teens attending. Every year, BFF offers totally free of charge BFF Exchange at Kickstarter, a full day of panels, seminars, and film pitching. Throughout the year, BFF offers a number of screenings at various Brooklyn venues/neighborhoods free of change. Although the full schedule is not available yet, it is already confirmed a BFF Showcase at the Brooklyn Museum on December 1, 2018 as part of the Museum's First Saturdays events. This event will be free of charge and the audience will be able to meet the filmmakers after the screenings.
